Portfolio

My portfolio project began by defining what my brand is and which design elements would best reflect my brand's personality, mood, and tone including color scheme and typography. I then considered UX and UI design creating a wireframe and PSD for my website using Photoshop. In Illustrator and InDesign I fashioned a logo and business card.

I brought all that design work to life coding my site using HTML and CSS. I chose and registerd a domain name and lauched the site on a webserver. This project was the culmination of Phase 1 of my Skillcrush coursework.
